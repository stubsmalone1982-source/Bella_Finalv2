Bella Project — Spine Spec (Build-Ready)

Full breakdown of the locked arc: visuals, audio, motion physics, interactions, and scene-by-scene choreography.

Purpose

Create a cinematic interactive web experience crafted specifically for her. Not a website — choreography. Target runtime ~12 minutes.

  • Tone progression: Playful → Aware → Focused → Intimate → Warm
  • End state: choosing Let’s Hang Out feels inevitable but never pressured.

Core Dynamic

  • Mutual awareness: she knows what she’s doing; he knows she knows; he lets her.
  • Not dominance. Not trapping. Not accusatory.
  • Intimacy arrives via subtraction, not escalation.

Global Style Language

  • Curated gothic elegance (Victorian mood, charcoal base, plum/violet ambience).
  • Muted antique silver typography (never pure white).
  • Animation: crossfades over cuts; emerging over appearing; motion must feel physical (light/focus/weight), not UI.

Audio System (Locked)

Background ambience (loop)

  • Starts on first click (Act I → Act II). Loops continuously.
  • Slight dip during Act II→Act III crossfade; slight additional dip after “You tilt your head” fades.
  • On “Let’s Hang Out” click: fade out 1.5–2s, then silence.

Giggle

  • Plays once, after “You bite your finger…” with a half-beat pause.
  • Volume slightly lower than expected (intimate).

Voice

  • Act III: “You bite your finger…” and “There it is.”
  • Final transition: “There you are… It’s good to see you, sweetheart.”
  • Delivery: calm, close, slight smile; not performed.

Act I — Threshold

Screen: pure black
Text: “Ready” (statement)
Button: “Let’s go”
  • On click: collapse inward → subtle violet compression → brief wormhole bridge (refined, not sci-fi) → expansion into Act II.
  • Music begins at collapse (low swell), fully present by arrival.

Act II — The World

Environment Layers

1) Deep charcoal base (not pure black) 2) Damask texture (low opacity, velvet feel) 3) Plum glow (wide, subtle, slightly below center) 4) Pentagram watermark (very faint, large, slightly above center, rotated ~10:30, etched feel)

World Engine Motion

  • Parallax: ultra-subtle depth shifts; damask least, glow more, pentagram slightly more; smooth easing.
  • Ripple: triggers after mouse pause (250–350ms); origin offset behind motion direction; velvet/light distortion; 600–800ms; no visible rings.
  • Intensity scaling: Phase 1=100%, Phase 2=80%, Phase 3=60%, Stillness=0% (freeze).

Act II Phase 1 — Discovery Words

  • Hidden words embedded in environment, revealed by ripple pass, then clickable:
  • mischievous (lower-right), playful (upper-right), tease (mid-left)
  • On click: word disappears from environment, appears top-center for 1.5–2s, fades out (no stacking).

Act II Phase 2 — Steering

I know that look. (beat) You’re having fun… (half beat) So… (half beat) …are we behaving?
  • Choice appears: Yes / No
  • Yes → “Oh really.” | No → “I thought so.” (tone branch only; converge back)

Act II Phase 3 — Convergence

Then you should follow me… (morph) …to the end of the journey.
  • Visual: center gravity shift / narrowing focus (not a portal, not blur-wipe).
  • Motion intensity reduces further; world calms.

Stillness Gate

  • Text emerges from dark center: Don’t move.
  • On success: ripple+parallax fade to 0; perceptible freeze.
  • “Good.” appears briefly (~0.8s) then crossfade into Act III begins.

Act III — Intimacy

  • Pure black background. Portrait fades in during crossfade; thin antique silver vine frame; subtle violet underglow; slow linear zoom.
  • Pause 2.5–3 seconds after portrait settles.
  • Text: “You get quiet.” then “You tilt your head.”
  • After “You tilt your head” fades: slight music dip.
  • Voice: “You bite your finger…” → half-beat → giggle → voice: “There it is.”

Final Fork

So… What do we do now?
  • Portrait crossfades out; buttons fade in horizontal, slightly below center.
  • Left: Let’s Hang Out | Right: One More Game
  • Hover: highlight hovered only; never dim the other.
  • Click: subtle inward pulse + violet deepen → fade to black → music fade out.

Final Transition Page (Let’s Hang Out path)

  • Pure black. Silence.
  • Voice: “There you are… It’s good to see you, sweetheart.”
  • Link appears as centered text (no button border); hover = slight brightness lift.

Build Order (Anti-Drift)

  1. World engine: canvas, parallax, ripple shader, intensity scalar, freeze
  2. Hidden word reveal/click/ack system
  3. Phase controller (1→2→3) tied to intensity + text entrances
  4. Stillness gate + crossfade into Act III
  5. Portrait stage + frame + slow zoom
  6. Audio cues (music start/dips/fade; giggle once; voice lines)
  7. Fork + final transition page

End of spec.
